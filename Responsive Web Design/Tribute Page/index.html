Lionel Messi

The magician of the game

Here's a timeline of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 1987 - Birth
  • 1994 - Joined Neville's Old Boys
  • 2000 - Signed by Barca Youth
  • 2002 - La Masia
  • 2003 - Snub Spain over Argentina
  • 2004 - League Debut and League Winners
  • 2005 - First Goal, first contract, international debut
  • 2006 - Champions League victory
  • 2007 - Hat-trick in Clasico, Goal of the Century
  • 2008 - Won the Summer Olympics with Argentina, handed the No.10 at Barcelona, Runner-Up for the Ballon d'Or
  • 2009 - Handed the No.10 at Argentina, Champions League Winners, Sextuple, Ballon D'or Winner, World Player of the Year
  • 2010 - Second consecutive Ballon d'or
  • 2011 - Champions League Winners, Third consecutive Ballon d'or
  • 2012 - Most goals in a calendar year, 5 goals in a Champions League match, top goalscorer in Barcelona's history, Fourth consectutive Ballon d'or
  • 2014 - All-time top scorer in El Clásico, Another treble World Cup Runner-Up with Argentina
  • 2015 - Champions League Winners, Runner-Up at Copa America, Domestic double, Fifth Ballon d'or
  • 2016 - Runner-Up at Copa America, guilty of tax-fraud, wins Pichichi and European Golden Boot for the fourth time, Announces retirement from International Football and returns to play for the National team
  • 2017 - Hat-trick propels Argentina into World Cup, Domestic double, La Remontada, Wins record fifth Pichichi Award
  • 2018 - Named Captain of the Barcelona team, tenth La Liga title, and a record sixth Golden Boot,, crashed out of Champions League by Roma's comeback and eliminated by France in the World Cup RO16
  • 2019 - Suffer defeat to liverpool after their comeback, Semi-finalists at the Copa America, League Winners, Scores 600th club goal for Barcelona, wins record sixth Ballon d'or
  • 2020 - Suffer crushing defeat to Bayern, scores 700th goal of his pro carrer, has most appearances at Barcelona, expresses desire to leave club

“What Leo does is so incredible that I have to be careful not to stand still watching him make his moves.”

- Thierry Henry, French football player
